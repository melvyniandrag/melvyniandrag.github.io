Shopping List

Power Supply 1. X(1) 1N4007 2. X(2) 220uF Caps 3. X(2) 100nF Caps 4. X(1) LM7805 5. Some means of providing 12V.

Pulse Counter 1. X(1) SN74LS14N 2. X(2) 1nF Caps
